In this group project, our team created a live VJ session for the song “The Final Catdown (Extended)” by REDCVT with our original concept below:

A severe pandemic has devastated the world, allowing animals to take control and divide humans into three distinct zones. Each zone is led by an animal representing one of three important virtues: Humanity, Decorum, and Uprightness. A mascot guides the audience through these zones to show what life is like for the animals that were once mistreated.

The leader of the zone is chosen by animals that have traits that are opposite to the name of the land:

The Humanity Zone: ruled by a rat, symbolizing darkness and cruelty.
The Decorum Zone: ruled by a monkey due to its mischievous nature and lack of respect for rules.
The Uprightness Zone: ruled by the ancient figure of justice, Anubis God, reminds us of fairness and moral strength.

RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

Concept & Research

I was responsible for the Project Research and contributed significantly to the Concept Development. I conducted the thematic research on animal symbolism and social hierarchy, creating the foundational lore for the three distinct zones.
